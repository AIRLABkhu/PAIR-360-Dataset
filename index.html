PAIR360 Dataset

  • This dataset provides 360 equirectangular image set with LiDAR point cloud from
    Insta360 pro2 - Velodyne VLP-32C system


Data

  • 360 equirectangular images (7680 x 3840)
    • RGB
    • Depth
  • Fish eye images (3840 x 2880)
    • RGB
  • LiDAR point clouds (32 channels)
  • Gyroscope and GPS

Weather condition

  • Sunny
  • Cloudy
  • Dawning

Area

  • We capture 7 areas for 3 times in the Kyunghee University International Campus
    • College of Engineering
    • College of Life Science
    • College of Physical Education
    • Central Library
    • 1st Dormitory
    • 2nd Dormitory
    • Parking Lot & Peace Amphitheater
